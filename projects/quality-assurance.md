# Annotation Quality Assurance

## Overview

Performed quality assurance and validation for large-scale image annotation projects to ensure consistency, accuracy, and compliance with annotation guidelines.

## Responsibilities

- Review completed annotations
- Identify labeling inconsistencies
- Correct annotation errors
- Validate dataset quality
- Follow project-specific annotation guidelines

## Skills Used

- Quality Assurance
- Data Validation
- Image Annotation
- Attention to Detail
- Guideline Compliance

## Note

Original datasets and internal quality guidelines cannot be shared publicly due to confidentiality agreements.
